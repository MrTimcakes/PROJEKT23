VisiBoard

PROJEKT23 is proud to present the VisiBoard. The worlds first "invisible" electric skateboard. Now we don't mean you can't see it, you can, we mean you can't see that it's an electric skateboard. We loved the versitilty exising electric skateboards gave us, but we absoultely hated their heft and limited maneuverability. That's where the VisiBoard comes in.

Our board is the first on the market to have a completely seamless electric design, our board looks exactly like any other regualr skateboard. Completely inconspicuous of the power it's packing. The VisiBoard contains four Twenty Kilowatt Brushless DC motors for lightning fast accelleration and an absoultely ridiculous Eight Hundred and Forty Kilowatt hour batter. All concealed within an identical to stock skateboard deck. We can guaruntee that the VisiBoard is unlike anything you've ever experienced.
